About Me

I'm a visual artist. A professional photographer. I believe every frame tells a story on it's own but also forms a piece of a puzzle to a bigger narrative.

My photography is mainly inspired by cinema (film), from capturing well planned-out shoots or scenes, to untampered-with sceneries like general human interactions (i.e street photography).

I'd say, nature photography and cinematic scenery got me into photography. Composing objects in a single frame to convey a certain feeling/emotion has always fascinated me, I saw it as a tool to tell my stories using single frames. Just over 4 years ago I decided to get myself a DSLR camera and I've been creating images ever since.

Every photoshoot is like a project on its own, it's unique and has a unique approach to it. What project are you working on?
